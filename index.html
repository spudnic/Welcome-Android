Application Flow

  1. Launch App, shows a splash screen 10 seconds that tells the user what to expect next.(Enter your Name, or a three word phrase)
  2. Display a text input and a button labeled process
  3. when you hit the button, it explodes from it's 2d into 3d slices
  4. Displays the phrase in 3d Text (optional letters fly in to place)
  5. Using the letters do a dictionary look up finding all the words that can be created with the letters from the names or phrase. (go to a animation screen while it is processing)
  6. Display a sorted list of all the words that can be created. ( each word flys in via 3d text zoomed out)
  7. The camera zooms in and the words scroll by.
  8. Then once the words have been scrolled display a time which is the time it took to process as well as fly through all the words.
